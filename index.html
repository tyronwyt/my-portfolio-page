About me

I was born in South Africa and love a good braai .

I enjoy all things digital and the Internet of things. If I am not building websites and learning new things, I am tinkering away on my 3d printer and making things. With a strong background of design, I enjoy bringing creativity in to my development process and always passionate to learn more along the way.

Following my passion, I left flight school to learn web development and on the way started facilitating at a national college in South Africa. I then moved on and joined an advertising startup where I was responsible for all the design and web work, whilst at the same time freelancing, building and deploying multiple WordPress sites. My latest experience includes working with a global creative team developing branding and marketing and communication material for a Fortune 500 company.
